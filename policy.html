Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how Orbit Vector AR, developed by four students of UTBM as a class project, collects, uses, and protects your information. The Game and its server are privately hosted.

1. Information We Collect

We collect and store the following information:

  • Your App Set ID (or a randomly generated UUID if unavailable)
  • Your chosen username
  • Your game data (score, money, unlocked content)
No other personal information is collected.

2. Use of Information

The information collected is used solely to provide game features such as user registration, leaderboards, and personal statistics. Your data will not be shared with third parties.

3. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored securely on a server hosted by the project team and is accessible only to authorized team members for the purposes described above.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

Your data will be retained as long as you use the Game. You may request deletion of your data at any time by visiting this page.

5. Children's Privacy

The Game does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with information, please contact us.

6. Changes to this Policy

The developers may update this Privacy Policy as necessary. Continued use of the Game constitutes acceptance of any changes.
