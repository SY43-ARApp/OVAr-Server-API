Request Deletion of Your Data

To permanently delete your Orbit Vector AR account and all associated data, please follow the steps below:

Step 1: Open the Orbit Vector AR app and go to the Main Menu screen.
Step 2: Tap the Copy UUID button to copy your unique user identifier (UUID) to your clipboard.
Step 3: Paste your UUID into the field below and submit the form to request deletion of your data.

If you have any questions or need assistance, please contact: fallingit.assistance@gmail.com
